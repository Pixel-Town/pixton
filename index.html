Field log · entry 001

A small town that runs
whether or not you watch.

Six residents. Twenty-five by eighteen tiles. They wander, bump into each other, mutter through their own private hangups, and keep going on their own clock. Pixton is the window — the brains plug in underneath, swappable, mock or live.
